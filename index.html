school achievements

Inter School Debate On Olympics
Won prize on Inter School Debate on Olympics

We had a MUN(Model United Nations) on Olympics. Delecates(Participants) were given different countries and has to say small speeches on conditions and the future of Olympics in there country.Our school won the best Delecate award.

Inter School Badminton Competition
Came third in the Inter School Badminton Competition.

Different age groups were representing the school, most of them got a participation certificate but U/12 division got a bronze medal.

Inter School Science Quiz and Debate
Won third prize in Inter school Science Debate and 5th in Quiz.

Grade 7 were taking part in the quiz and grade 8 in the debate. Both grade 7 and 8 had 1 team with 2 people. Grade 8th had to work in a team while Grade 7th had to alone. Both participants in 8th grade got 3rd place and 1 participant in grade 7 got 5th place.
